We have founded a new nonprofit organization, Prion Alliance, devoted to finding a treatment or cure for human prion diseases.  Check out our brand new website at PrionAlliance.org and let us know what you think.  Going forward, PrionAlliance.org will be the organization’s official website, and CureFFI.org will continue to be an informal forum for discussing the latest research and sharing ideas and methodologies.
